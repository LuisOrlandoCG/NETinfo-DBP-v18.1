Mass Payments

Mass Transaction Status

End-users can upload their payment files (in CSV or SEPA-XML format) and submit them for execution to the Core System in real time or at a future date.  The business rules (e.g. account validations, access rights, limits, credit check) that apply for standard Fund Transfers are applied before submission. For non-SEPA XML file formats, additional validations as defined by the bank administrator in the Admin console apply.

Mass Authorisations

End-users, assigned as approvers/signatories, may view the mass payments that are pending approval on them, and approve or decline them as required (the option to approve/decline individual payment instructions within the file is available, provided that all payment instructions in the file are processed)
