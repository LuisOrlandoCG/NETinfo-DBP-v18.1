NETinfo DBP Version 18.1 : Fund Transfers and Payments

Created by Fivi Konstantinou, last modified by 1057-Michael Philippos on 04, 07, 2024

Fund Transfers

Bill Payments

End-users can initiate a money transfer  from one of their ‘connected accounts’ to any Bank account, local or international. Cross currency transfers and end-user approvals (signatures) are also supported. The payment instruction can be submitted (to the Core System) for execution in real time or at a future date or as a standing order.

Know More >>>

End-users can pay their bills (e.g. Telephone Bills, Electricity Bills, Taxes, School fees), payroll and suppliers online. The Bank is given the capability to create/amend/delete Payment Templates as per the requirements of their customers.

Know More >>>

Quick Pay

Card Payments

End-users can register and use the quick pay service for easy payments to/from other registered for the service end-users. When making a Quick Pay payment, the end-user may select the beneficiary by entering their mobile number or selecting them from their phone’s contact list.

Know More >>>

End-users can top-up their cards or make a payment to another card within the Bank. When toping up their cards, they have the option to pay the full or minimum amount (these amounts are retrieved from Core System) or specify a custom amount. The payment instruction is submitted to the Core System for execution in real time.

Know More >>>

Negotiated Rates

Standing Orders (Maintained by NETinfo DBP)

End-users can request for a special exchange rate (offline) when making cross-currency money transfers. The request is processed by Bank through the NETinfo DBP and, if approved, end-users receive a special token. This is used for applying the agreed exchange rate (negotiated rate) when initiating the money transfer.

Know More >>>

End-users can view/create/amend/cancel their own standing orders (and future payments) online using the NETinfo scheduler. The standing order (future payment) is executed by the bank’s back-end system.

Know More >>>

Standing Orders (Maintained by Core System)

Remote Cheque Deposit

End-users can view their Standing Orders as maintained by the Core System and submit instructions for creating new & amending/cancelling existing Standing Orders.

Know More >>>

End-users may deposit their cheques through their mobile app without visiting a local branch. The ‘Remote Cheque Deposit’ request from the end-user may be viewed and processed through the NETinfo Admin Console.

Know More >>>

Beneficiaries

Charges

End-users can create/view/amend/delete beneficiaries. One beneficiary (individual or company) may have multiple beneficiary accounts connected, each account held by different bank (either Domestic or International bank). Beneficiaries are linked to a bank customer thereby allowing multiple end-users (with the associated privileges) to use/view/amend/delete them if/as required (configurable).

Know More >>>

Define rules relating to charges/fees. Each rule defines the amount or percentage fee that will be applied at transaction type level, channel level , transfer amount range and currency level

Know More >>>

Online Transaction Status

User Approvals

End-users can view the status of transactions/requests initiated from the NETinfo digital channels for selected accounts or customers. Typical information displayed: date, transaction details, status and approval details. End-users are also provided with the option to cancel a transaction (in case it has not been processed yet) or repeat it (i.e. reload transaction details for resubmission)

Know More >>>

Set up complex signatory structures: Define signatory groups (e.g. A, B, C), assign a end-user to a signatory group for a specific account (i.e. make an end-user a ‘signatory’), define a signature rule at account and transaction type level, support sequential or non-sequential workflows for the approvals.

Know More >>>

Mass Payments

Payroll Payments

End-users can upload their payment files (in CSV or SEPA-XML format), submit them to the Core System for execution and monitor their processing status thereafter. User approval workflows (signatures) are supported.

Know More >>>

End-users can create & manage a registry of their employees (with associated payroll related information), prepare payroll templates, schedule their submission to the Core System for execution and monitor their processing status thereafter. User approval workflows (signatures) are supported.
